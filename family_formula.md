# Family Wealth Formula: A Blueprint for All Families

## **Version: 0.0.1**

### **Date: 2025-09-15**

This is not an application or a service. It is a simple financial formula designed for any family to implement. It can be managed on a spreadsheet, in a notebook, or with a simple script.

## The Core Formula

The goal is to create generational wealth using a simple, daily process for each child.

### Step 1: Daily Allocation

* For every child, allocate **$2 per day**. This amount serves as the base for all further calculations.

### Step 2: First Dollar Distribution

* Take the first **$1**.
* Split this dollar for the next three generations. This can be an even split or based on a family's own defined percentages.

### Step 3: Second Dollar Distribution

* Take the second **$1**.
* Split this dollar in half:
    * **$0.50** is given to the child.
    * **$0.50** is allocated to the child's "wants" account for discretionary spending.

### Step 4: The Family Share

* From the child's daily allocation, return **1/10th** of the total amount back to the family's shared account.

### Step 5: The Wealth Accelerator

This step is designed to turn a child's spending into a mechanism for building generational wealth.

* When a child spends money from their "wants" account, a parent pays that debt immediately by paying double the amount spent.
* This double-paid amount is then taken from the child's allocation and put directly into the **first family's generational wealth account**.

This process ensures that a child's spending is always tied to the growth of the family's shared wealth, creating a cycle of abundance.
